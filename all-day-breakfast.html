LOCAL FAVORITE

Sweet Palm Coffee

Location: King Street, Downtown Charleston

Located on the vibrant King Street corridor, Sweet Palm Coffee has become a beloved destination for all day breakfast in downtown Charleston. Their commitment to serving breakfast from morning until close sets them apart from traditional cafes. The menu features classic breakfast staples alongside creative variations, all prepared with locally sourced ingredients when possible. From fluffy scrambled eggs and crispy bacon to Belgian waffles and fresh fruit parfaits, every item maintains the same high quality whether you order at 8 AM or 8 PM. The welcoming atmosphere and consistently excellent service make it an ideal spot for both quick breakfast bites and leisurely morning-style meals enjoyed in the afternoon. Their grab and go options also make it convenient for those on the move who still want a quality breakfast experience.

Visit Sweet Palm Coffee

Toast! All Day

Location: Cannon Street, Downtown

True to its name, Toast! specializes in all day breakfast with a menu that celebrates morning classics with creative twists. Their extensive breakfast offerings include everything from traditional eggs benedict to innovative breakfast tacos and grain bowls. The casual, contemporary atmosphere appeals to both families and young professionals seeking quality breakfast fare beyond traditional morning hours.

Millers All Day

Location: Spring Street, Downtown

Millers All Day lives up to its name with a comprehensive breakfast menu available throughout their operating hours. Known for generous portions and Southern hospitality, this local favorite serves up everything from buttermilk pancakes to shrimp and grits. Their commitment to quality ingredients and scratch cooking ensures every breakfast plate meets high standards regardless of when you order.

Basic Kitchen

Location: Upper King Street

Basic Kitchen offers an all day breakfast menu that emphasizes fresh, wholesome ingredients. Their breakfast selections range from hearty omelets to lighter options like avocado toast and acai bowls. The bright, modern space and health-conscious menu appeal to those seeking nutritious breakfast choices available beyond morning hours.

Hominy Grill

Location: Rutledge Avenue (Near Downtown)

A Charleston institution, Hominy Grill serves breakfast all day with a focus on Lowcountry classics. Their famous shrimp and grits, biscuits with sausage gravy, and country-style breakfast platters showcase authentic Southern cooking. The charming, cozy atmosphere adds to the appeal of enjoying breakfast favorites at any hour.

Page's Okra Grill

Location: Folly Road (Short drive from downtown)

Page's Okra Grill brings Southern comfort to all day breakfast with generous portions and authentic Lowcountry flavors. Their breakfast menu includes traditional favorites like country ham with red eye gravy, fried chicken and waffles, and various egg platters. The down-home atmosphere and reasonable prices make it a local favorite for breakfast at any time.
